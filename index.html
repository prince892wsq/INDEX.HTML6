PRINCE Weblog: Discovering Filipino Food

Welcome to my journey exploring the rich and flavorful world of Filipino cuisine!

Adobo – A Classic Favorite

Adobo is one of the most popular dishes in the Philippines. It's a savory blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. You can make it with chicken, pork, or even vegetables. It’s easy to make and full of flavor!

Sinigang – A Comforting Sour Soup

Sinigang is a sour tamarind-based soup that's perfect for rainy days. It usually includes pork, shrimp, or fish with vegetables like kangkong, radish, and okra. It’s tangy, refreshing, and comforting all at once.

Halo-Halo – A Sweet Summer Treat

Halo-halo is a colorful dessert made with crushed ice, sweet beans, jellies, fruits, leche flan, and ube ice cream. It’s perfect for hot days and a must-try for anyone new to Filipino desserts!
